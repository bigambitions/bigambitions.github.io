Founding Members

Big Ambitions Foundation is currently looking for its Founding Members / Sponsors to help achieve its mission.

Benefits of becoming a Founding Member

Leadership

  • One Member from the founding members would be represented to our Board of Advisors, who will have a say in making the key decisions of our organisation. The Board of Advisors help govern the foundation and will be meeting in person annually.
  • Our organisation will showcase the importance of the open source developers employed within our founding members company and impact being made by them to the open source technologies, featuring in our spotlight feature hence representing it to the larger community.

Brand Visibility

  • As being a founding member of our organisation, you will be given the brand visibility in all our meetups and training sessions.
  • Founding members logo would be present on the lanyards of the meetups and training sessions area.
  • Founding members gain brand visibility in all the presentations and talks our team might be giving at the open source conferences and summits.
  • Founding members logo along with a link to their website would be mentioned on the front page of our website and on the sponsors page with a short introduction about the company.
  • Founding members company logo would be present on our company T-shirt provided to people at meetups, trainings and conferences.
  • Founding members logo along with a short introduction would be present on our brochure and monthly newsletter.
  • Founding members logo would be present on the certificates given to the candidates on the successful completion of the training.

Talent Acquisition

  • Our organisation would be providing space on our website for our founding members to mention job opportunities they may have within their company.
  • Our organisation would also be providing access to our database of trained candidates to shortlist them for jobs within their company.

Marketing

  • Our organisation would also be helping our founding members in marketing of their new products and services via our social media presence on LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, Google+.
  • Our founding members have the opportunity to showcase their products, technologies and services at our meetup sessions.
  • We have a spotlight feature on our website for our founding members to market their open source products and technologies.
  • Our founding members would also be provided space in our monthly newsletter to market their products, services and technologies.
