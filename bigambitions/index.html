About Us

Tue, Dec 8, 2015

Big Ambitions Foundation feature news, updates, research and anaylsis as well as provides a common platform to share knowledge and latest updates about Open source Cloud, SDN/NFV, Linux, Containers and Networking technologies. The members of the Foundation analyse and perform data research to help the enterprises, startups and the businesses worldwide choose the right stack of open source technologies. Big Ambitions Foundation helps talented and passionate newcomers build their career in Open source, Linux, Cloud, SDN / NFV, Networking by providing comprehensive, practical and hands-on training to women and students at an affordable fees.

Founding Members

Fri, Dec 4, 2015

Big Ambitions Foundation is currently looking for its Founding Members / Sponsors to help achieve its mission. Benefits of becoming a Founding Member Leadership One Member from the founding members would be represented to our Board of Advisors, who will have a say in making the key decisions of our organisation. The Board of Advisors help govern the foundation and will be meeting in person annually. Our organisation will showcase the importance of the open source developers employed within our founding members company and impact being made by them to the open source technologies, featuring in our spotlight feature hence representing it to the larger community.
