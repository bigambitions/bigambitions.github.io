About Big Ambitions Foundation

Big Ambitions Foundation feature news, updates, research and anaylsis as well as provides a common platform to share knowledge and latest updates about Open source Cloud, SDN/NFV, Linux, Containers and Networking technologies. The members of the Foundation analyse and perform data research to help the enterprises, startups and the businesses worldwide choose the right stack of open source technologies.

Big Ambitions Foundation helps talented and passionate newcomers build their career in Open source, Linux, Cloud, SDN / NFV, Networking by providing comprehensive, practical and hands-on training to women and students at an affordable fees. The goal of the Big Ambitions Foundation is to bridge the gap between open source and women in technology.

Our Vision

  • Provide a common platform to share knowledge and updates on the open source technologies.
  • Help talented and passionate newcomers build their career in Open source, Linux, Cloud, SDN / NFV and Networking.
  • Bridge the gap between open source and women in technology.
  • Encourage students and women in technology to get involved in open source community and contribute upstream.
  • Help the enterprises, startups and the businesses worldwide choose the right stack of open source technologies.

Our Mission

  • Featuring tutorials on our focused open source technologies.
  • Analysing and performing data research to show how these new open source technologies would affect the enterprises, startups and the businesses worldwide.
  • Providing comprehensive, practical and hands-on training on our focused open source rechnologies to women and students at low cost.

Founding Members

We are looking for Founding Members / Sponsors to join our nonprofit organisation and help us in achieving our mission. If your company is interested in becoming a Founding Member/ Sponsor at Big Ambitions Foundation, Please send us an email to info (at) bigambitions (dot) org.

Advisory Board at Big Ambitions

Big Ambitions Foundation would be governed on the policies decided by our Board of Advisors council. Our Board of Advisors includes one member from each of the founding members / sponsors, and respected experienced persons from the open source community.

Focused technologies

Big Ambitions would be currently focusing on Open Soure Cloud, SDN / NFV and Networking technologies mainly Linux systems (Ubuntu, RHEL, CentOS), OpenStack, OpenDaylight, Docker and Linux containers, Kubernetes, Apache Mesos, OpenShift, Vagrant, Puppet.

More technologies to be added soon in future!

Benefit to the Open Source world

In our training sessions, we would be encouraging the participants to contribute upstream and get involved with the larger community. This would help the open source technologies to have more contributions and in effect will increase the number of people with open source experience in the market for the companies to hire open source talent.

Also, our foundation would be working to bridge the gap between Open source and women by offering training to women at low cost.

We would also be conducting free meetups worldwide to share knowledge and latest updates about the Open source technologies which would help the people and newbies in the open source world to enhance their skills as well as learn new skills.

Benefit to the Users

Passionate Women and Students would be having the opportunity to get hands-on training on the Open source technologies at no cost and hence can make their career in Cloud Computing, SDN / NFV, Linux and Networking.

Benefit to the Startups and Enterprises

Our team would be performing data research and analysis of the open source technologies, and will publish it on our website as well as via our social media presence which would help the startups, enterprises and the businesses worldwide to choose the right stack of open source technologies for building their product.
