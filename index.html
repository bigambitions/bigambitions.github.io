About Us

Big Ambitions is a nonprofit organisation dedicated to the sharing of knowledge and updates about Open source Cloud, SDN / NFV and Networking technologies mainly Linux systems, OpenStack, OpenDaylight, OPNFV, Docker and Linux containers, Flocker, Kubernetes, Mesos, OpenShift, Clair, Weave on a common platform.

Big Ambitions helps talented and passionate newcomers build their career in Open source, Linux, Cloud, SDN / NFV, Networking by providing comprehensive, practical training at no cost to women and at an affordable fees to the students worldwide. Big Ambitions has been founded to help close the gap between open source and women in technology.

Big Ambitions feature news, updates, research and anaylsis of the open source technologies, interviews with the developers and organise meetups to share knowledge on a common platform. We analyse and perform data research to show how the new open source technologies would affect the enterprises, startups and the businesses worldwide.
